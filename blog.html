Export list of installed programs

Published on: 2025-11-20

Get-ItemProperty HKLM:\Software\Wow6432Node\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Uninstall\* |Select-Object DisplayName, DisplayVersion | Export-Csv "C:\InstalledPrograms.csv" -NoTypeInformation

Check disk space

Published on: 2025-11-05

Get-PSDrive -PSProvider FileSystem | Select-Object Name, Free, Used, @{Name="Total";Expression={$_.Free + $_.Used}}

List all running processes

Published on: 2025-11-01

Get-Process | Sort-Object CPU -Descending | Select-Object -First 10
